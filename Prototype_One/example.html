Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I register as a patient with DrugDistro Company?

A1: To register as a patient, you can visit our website and fill out the patient registration form. Alternatively, you can also visit any of our partner pharmacies and complete the registration process in person.

Q2: Can I choose my preferred doctor?

A2: Yes, as a patient of DrugDistro Company, you have the freedom to choose your preferred doctor. During the registration process, you can indicate your preferred doctor, or you can request a recommendation from our team based on your needs.

Q3: How can I refill my prescription?

A3: Refilling your prescription is easy. Simply log in to your patient account on our website and submit a refill request. Alternatively, you can contact your primary physician or visit any of our partner pharmacies to request a prescription refill.

Q4: Can I transfer my prescription to another pharmacy?

A4: Yes, if you wish to transfer your prescription to another pharmacy, please contact our customer support team or visit the new pharmacy of your choice. They will assist you with the necessary steps to transfer your prescription.
